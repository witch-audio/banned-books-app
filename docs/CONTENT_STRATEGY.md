# Content Strategy

## Core content engines

1. Book pages
2. Challenge/location pages
3. Editorial list pages
4. Timely stories

## First content ideas

- 25 books people should know
- Most challenged books in America
- Why these books keep getting challenged
- Challenged books by state
- If you liked one challenged book, read these next

## Content goal

Every page should do at least one of these:
- rank
- get shared
- help someone act
- earn an outbound commerce click
