# Brand Notes

Working name: BannedBooks.app

Core hook:
- Discover the books people tried to erase.

Possible product descriptions:
- A reading-freedom tracker for challenged books.
- Find challenged books, see where they were targeted, and learn how to get them.
- A cultural tracker for books people tried to pull off shelves.

Brand traits:
- sharp
- literary
- useful
- alive
- current
- brave but not corny

Anti-goals:
- not dry academic database energy
- not generic activism branding
- not cluttered news-site chaos
